Grant Calls

DTC Singapore will be introducing a research excellence grant and an innovation grant to encourage the advancement of trust technologies and innovative solutions that address real-world challenges, aiming to establish a secure, transparent, and accountable digital environment, while contributing to national benefits for Singapore.

The proposed opportunities and focus areas will encompass, but are not limited to, the following areas:

  • Trusted Analysis

    Gaining insights while ensuring the protection and preservation of data privacy

  • Trusted Identity

    Emerging domains of trust in verification and authentication

  • Trusted Compute

    In distributed architectures and heterogeneous platforms

  • Trusted Accreditation

    Technologies for evaluation and auditing to validate trustworthy products and services
